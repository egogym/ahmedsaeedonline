Program Overview

This 4-week weight loss program combines strength training and cardio for optimal fat loss results. Training days are Saturday, Sunday, Monday, and Wednesday, with built-in recovery days. Each session includes a proper warm-up and cool-down to prevent injury and promote recovery.

Training Guidelines

Key Training Points

  • • Rest 60-90 seconds between sets
  • • Perform proper warm-up before each session
  • • Focus on form over weight/speed
  • • Progressive overload when possible
  • • Stay hydrated during workouts
  • • Listen to your body and adjust as needed

Recovery & Nutrition

  • • Maintain a balanced diet with adequate protein
  • • Get 7-8 hours of sleep per night
  • • Stay in a moderate caloric deficit
  • • Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily
  • • Consider light mobility work on rest days
  • • Track your progress weekly
